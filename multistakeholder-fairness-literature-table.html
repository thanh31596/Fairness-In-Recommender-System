Key Insights from Literature Analysis

  • Evolution: Field has progressed from single to multi-stakeholder perspectives, but behavioral complexity remains limited
  • Technical Focus: Heavy emphasis on optimization algorithms, less on stakeholder behavior and dynamics
  • Static Assumptions: Most work assumes fixed preferences, utilities, and roles
  • Validation Gap: Limited behavioral validation of fairness mechanisms
  • Opportunity: Generative agents can address all major limitations through realistic behavioral simulation
